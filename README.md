# MultiObjRobustOptimization
from https://doi.org/10.56850/jnse.1478848
"ROBUST PLANNING OF IRRIGATION CONSIDERING WATER CONSUMPTION AND REVENUE ": Water scarcity is a problem for many regions which requires immediate action, and solutions cannot be postponed for a long time. It is known that farming consumes a significant portion of usable water. In this study, a decision support model of bi-objective stochastic linear formulation is proposed. The model is generating annual planting plans together with water consumption projections for each farmer in the region while taking revenue of the overall harvest into account. The structure of the proposed model maintains robustness against the volatilities in precipitation, yield, and market price. The inherent trade-off between water consumption and revenue lends itself to multi-objective planning..."
